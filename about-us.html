About Us

The name 단밤(danbam) comes from 단 (dan), which is a form of the adjective 달다 (dalda), meaning sweet, and 밤 (bam) which means night.

In the webtoon, the pub was named 꿀밤 (kkulbam), which means honey night. The drama chose to change the name to a very close substitute, danbam, because the real life writer of the webtoon actually opened a pub named kkulbam, just like in the webtoon

History

After Park Sae-royi spent seven years deep fishing, he opened Danbam, his first restaurant in Itaewon. In real life, Danbam's first restaurant was formerly known as Undukjib, a gopchang (tripe) restaurant. According to The Saranghe Opa, a K-Drama fan page, this location is quite easy to spot!
